About Us

McEvoy Drywall

About Company Title

Mastering the art of drywall repair and finishing has been our passion and expertise for years. Beyond simple repairs, our seasoned team specializes in tasks that truly transform a room, such as removing dated popcorn ceilings and applying a smooth skim coat.

Over the decades, countless satisfied customers can attest to the meticulous care and precision we bring to every project. Yet, in this industry, craftsmanship is just one side of the coin. When choosing a contractor, trust is paramount. Our reputation as a third-generation family business stands not only on the impeccable quality of our work but also on the trust and integrity we've fostered with our clients.

From day one, we've focused on stellar customer service. That's why our customers call us time and again. If you have a question or concern, you can speak directly to me any time. We pride ourselves on our fast, friendly customer care.

Kieran McEvoy Founder
